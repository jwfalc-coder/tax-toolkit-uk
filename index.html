About Tax Toolkit UK

Built for people who just want a straight answer

Most free tax calculators are either too simple to be useful or buried under paywalls and registration walls. Tax Toolkit UK is different: every calculator is free, immediate, and built on HMRC's actual published rates for 2025/26.

All calculations happen locally in your browser. No data leaves your device, no account is required, and no figure you enter is stored or shared. The results are guidance, so always verify anything financially significant with a qualified accountant or tax adviser.
